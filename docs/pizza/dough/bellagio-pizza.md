## Ingredients
Amount | Ingredient
--- | ---
480g | flour
9oz | water
17g | salt
17g | olive oil
3g | active yeast

## Method
1. Straight dough method
2. Cover and proof until double in size \(About 60 minutes\)
3. Punch down, roll into a ball cover lightly in olive oil wrap and proof in fridge for 12\-24 hours
